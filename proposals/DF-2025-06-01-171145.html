Programme Overview

This premium Provence experience has been designed for discerning travelers seeking an authentic immersion into the region's cultural heritage, viticultural excellence, and gastronomic traditions. Based in historic Avignon, your clients will explore the sun-drenched landscapes of the Luberon, discover UNESCO-protected monuments, and taste exceptional wines in the legendary Châteauneuf-du-Pape appellation.

Curated Experiences Included

Private Luberon Villages Tour

Full-day exploration of Gordes and Roussillon, two of Provence's most enchanting hilltop villages. Wander cobblestone streets, discover local artisan workshops, and capture breathtaking panoramic views. Includes lunch at a traditional restaurant with regional specialties.

Châteauneuf-du-Pape Wine Tasting Experience

Private guided tour through prestigious estates in this legendary wine region. Meet winemakers, explore historic cellars, and taste premium cuvées. Your expert guide will share insights into the unique terroir and winemaking traditions that make these wines world-renowned.

Private Avignon Historical Walking Tour

Intimate exploration of Avignon's rich papal heritage with a specialized local guide. Visit the imposing Palais des Papes, stroll the charming old town, and discover hidden squares and medieval architecture. Tour adapted to your clients' interests in history and culture.
