Programme

Journée complète · Retour 18:30
This curated itinerary addresses your clients' sophisticated profile: James' serious wine collection background and Sarah's passion for contemporary art and authentic markets. We've designed a route that avoids tourist traps and focuses on producer relationships, artistic heritage, and the real Provence rhythm they're seeking.
  • 09:00 Départ du Couvent des Minimes avec guide expert et véhicule premium (sedan ou SUV selon préférence à confirmer)
  • 09:45 Marché local authentique — Stop at a genuine Provençal market (Apt, Lourmarin, or Coustellet depending on day) where Sarah can experience local life, artisan producers, and seasonal products. Your guide will introduce key vendors and help navigate the edible landscape.
  • 11:00 Domaine viticole sérieux — Private visit and tasting at a respected family estate with serious terroir credentials (not a commercial cave). Options include Domaine de la Citadelle, Château la Canorgue, or Bastide du Claux depending on availability. Extended tasting with winemaker or cellar master. James will appreciate the technical conversation and cellar architecture.
  • 13:15 Déjeuner gastronomique avec vue — Lunch at a refined table with panoramic Luberon views. Options: La Bastide de Gordes, Le Carillon (Goult), or Auberge de la Loube. Menu adapted for Sarah (no red meat). Focus on local producers, seasonal vegetables, and exceptional wine list.
  • 15:30 Village perché & patrimoine artistique — Gordes or Roussillon exploration with focus on artistic heritage (Vasarely Foundation in Gordes, ochre quarries and artist studios in Roussillon). Your guide will contextualize the region's impact on 20th-century art movements and contemporary scene.
  • 17:00 Pause panoramique & dégustation — Final wine stop or artisan producer visit (olive oil, cheese, or honey depending on route and interest). Time to breathe, photograph, and absorb the landscape that inspired Cézanne and Camus.
  • 18:30 Retour au Couvent des Minimes
