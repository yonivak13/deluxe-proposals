Programme Suggestions

Private Luberon Wine & Villages Full Day Tour

A perfectly curated day for serious wine collectors and art enthusiasts. This private experience takes you beyond the typical tourist trail into the authentic heart of Luberon, where contemporary galleries meet centuries-old wine estates. Designed specifically for the Hendersons' first Provence experience, building on their Burgundy and Tuscany knowledge.

  • Gordes: Private morning exploration of this iconic hilltop village, including lesser-known contemporary art galleries and the weekly artisan market (if Thursday)
  • Exclusive wine estate visit: Private tasting at a prestigious Luberon appellation property with exceptional rosés and whites, with focus on terroir discussion and collector-worthy vintages for James
  • Gourmet lunch: Refined Provençal cuisine at a hidden gem restaurant, with vegetarian and seafood options for Sarah
  • Les Baux-de-Provence or Roussillon: Afternoon visit to ochre cliffs and art ateliers, or the dramatic medieval village of Les Baux with its contemporary art exhibitions at Carrières de Lumières
  • Second winery visit: Boutique organic producer specializing in natural wines and innovative winemaking techniques
  • Return to Le Couvent des Minimes by 18:30

Provence Wine & Gastronomy Discovery Tour

An alternative focusing more intensively on wine and culinary traditions. Perfect for James's collector interests while ensuring Sarah's dietary preferences are beautifully accommodated. This option emphasizes food-wine pairings and includes a cooking demonstration.

  • Morning market visit: Private guided tour of Apt or Lourmarin market with a local chef, selecting ingredients for a later cooking demonstration
  • Premier wine estate: Extended private tasting at a renowned Luberon château with vertical tastings and cellar tour
  • Hands-on culinary experience: Interactive cooking class or demonstration featuring Provençal specialties adapted to Sarah's preferences (no red meat)
  • Gourmet lunch: Enjoy the prepared dishes paired with estate wines
  • Afternoon village exploration: Ménerbes or Bonnieux, with artisan visits (olive oil producer, pottery, lavender distillery)
  • Final wine stop: Hidden-gem vineyard producing small-batch wines rarely exported
  • Return by 18:30

Authentic Provence: Gordes, Wineries & Local Markets

The most balanced option combining all the Hendersons' interests with authentic local encounters. This itinerary provides the perfect introduction to Provence for clients who have experienced Burgundy and Tuscany, offering comparative wine knowledge while discovering Provence's unique cultural landscape.

  • Gordes exploration: Morning discovery of this stunning perched village, contemporary art galleries, and the 12th-century Village des Bories (ancient stone dwellings)
  • Abbey de Sénanque: Visit to the iconic lavender abbey (subject to seasonal opening), a photographer's dream and cultural landmark
  • First wine estate: Private tasting at a family-owned domaine with focus on Luberon AOC terroir and collector bottles
  • Authentic Provençal lunch: Seasonal menu at a village bistro or estate restaurant, accommodating Sarah's dietary preferences
  • Roussillon ochre village: Afternoon exploration of dramatic ochre cliffs and artist studios, with time for contemporary art browsing
  • Second winery visit: Biodynamic or innovative producer offering contrasting style to morning estate
  • Local artisan stop: Goat cheese producer, olive oil mill, or lavender distillery depending on season and interest
  • Return to hotel by 18:30
